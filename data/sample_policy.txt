Company Annual Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

Annual leave must be requested at least 14 days in advance through the HR portal.

Unused annual leave may be carried forward, but only up to 7 days.

Carried-forward leave must be used within the first three months of the next calendar year.

Employees on probation are entitled to annual leave on a pro-rata basis.

Annual leave requests may be declined during critical business periods.
